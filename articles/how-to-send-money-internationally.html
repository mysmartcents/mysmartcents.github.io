How to Send Money Internationally — Cheapest Methods 2026

SmartCents Editorial Team  ·  6 min read  ·  Updated 2026

Millions of people send money across borders every day — workers supporting families back home, businesses paying international suppliers, travelers topping up accounts abroad. Most people lose significant money to hidden fees and poor exchange rates without realizing it.

The Real Cost of Sending Money

Transfer Fees — A flat fee or percentage charged for processing. The visible cost most people see.

Exchange Rate Margin — The difference between the real mid-market rate and what the provider offers you. This hidden cost is often much larger than the transfer fee.

Always calculate the total cost — fees plus exchange rate margin — before choosing a provider.

Best Methods to Send Money Internationally

Bank Wire Transfer — Traditional, available everywhere but usually the most expensive. Takes 3–5 business days.

Online Money Transfer Services — Faster at competitive rates, with multiple delivery options including bank deposit, cash pickup, and mobile wallet.

Cryptocurrency — Increasingly popular especially in countries with limited banking access. Low fees but requires comfort with crypto.

Tips to Get the Best Exchange Rate

  • Always compare rates before sending — even small differences add up
  • Avoid airport and hotel exchange counters — worst rates
  • Send larger amounts less frequently to reduce fixed fees
  • Check the mid-market rate on Google first, then compare providers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest way to send money internationally?

It depends on the corridor, the amount, and delivery method. Online transfer services generally offer better rates than banks. Always compare before sending.

How long does an international transfer take?

Bank transfers take 3–5 business days. Most online services deliver within minutes to 24 hours depending on the destination.

Is it safe to send money online?

Yes, when using regulated and established providers. Always use services licensed in your country with strong customer reviews.
